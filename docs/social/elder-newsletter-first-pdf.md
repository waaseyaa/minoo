I've been building Minoo (minoo.live) — a website where Anishinaabe events, Teachings, language, and community news from across the North Shore and Manitoulin live in one place. Yesterday I got the print side working. The PDF on the left is generated from the same events that show on the website (right). The point: Elders and Knowledge Keepers who don't use the internet still get the news — printed, mailed, or dropped off where people gather.

Issue 1 is rough. One page, a handful of events, one Ojibwe word under "Language." The pipeline works though, and that was the hard part. Content will grow. If you have events, Teachings, or stories you'd like in future issues, send them my way.

About the name: "Manitoulin Regional" is a placeholder. Drop suggestions in the comments — Anishinaabemowin, English, or mixed. Elders will have the final say on what gets picked.
